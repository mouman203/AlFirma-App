# 🌾 AL Firma

### Smart Agricultural Marketplace & Advisory Platform

**AL Firma** is a mobile application developed with **Flutter** that connects **farmers, veterinarians, and agricultural customers** on a single platform.

The application aims to **digitalize agricultural services** by enabling farmers to sell products, access agricultural services, consult veterinary experts, and benefit from intelligent tools such as **recommendation systems** and **AI-powered plant disease detection**.

---

# 🚀 Key Features

## 🧑‍🌾 Agricultural Marketplace

Farmers can publish and sell agricultural products such as:

* Vegetables
* Fruits
* Livestock
* Other farm products

Customers can easily browse available products and contact sellers.

---

## 🛠 Agricultural Services Marketplace

The platform also provides a variety of **agriculture-related services** to support farmers and producers.

Available services include:

* ❄️ **Cold Storage Rental (Chambre Froid)** – Farmers can rent cold storage spaces to preserve their products.
* 🔧 **Equipment Repair Services** – Access technicians who repair agricultural machinery.
* 👷 **Hire Agricultural Workers** – Farmers can find workers for seasonal or temporary tasks.
* 🚚 **Transportation Services** – Transport goods between farms, markets, and storage facilities.
* 📊 **Agricultural Expertise & Consulting** – Professional advice from agricultural experts.

---

## 🩺 Veterinary Services

The platform allows **veterinarians to offer consultations and services**, helping farmers and livestock owners access professional veterinary assistance.

Features include:

* Veterinary profiles
* Service listings
* Direct communication with farmers

---

## 🤖 Smart Recommendation System

AL Firma integrates a **hybrid recommendation system** that suggests relevant agricultural products to users based on their interaction history and product attributes.

This improves the **shopping experience** and helps customers discover relevant products from local farmers.

---

## 🌿 AI Plant Disease Detection

The app integrates an **AI model capable of detecting plant diseases from images**.

Users can:

1. Take or upload a photo of a plant
2. The AI analyzes the image
3. The system predicts potential plant diseases

This helps farmers **identify plant health issues early and take appropriate action**.
